Elijah Lawrence

Hi! I'm Eli, a fourth year premed at UC San Diego, currently pursuing a BS in Biochemistry and Cellular biology. My interests include public health and research, and I love to teach. I currently work as an ACT/SAT and AP Biology tutor at Pivot Tutors. In addition, I am also an undergraduate instructional assistant for a metabolic biochemistry course. As someone who loves giving back to my commnuity, I worked as a Pathmaker Intern at Palomar Hospital, Escondido for almost two years. At the UC San Diego School of Medicine, I work in a genomics research lab that studies high-altitude populations and the human response to low oxygen. In my downtime, I love to hike, draw, and play the guitar.

Ethan Lawrence

I’m a fourth-year undergraduate student at The University of California, Santa Barbara receiving a BA in Western Music Studies. My primary interest lies in composing and writing music, from smaller ensemble pieces in line with the western art music tradition to more pop-oriented lyrical songs that exist in a less formal environment. I have considerable experience with playing the piano and utilizing it as a tool for composing. I’m largely inspired by the works of film composers such as Joe Hisaishi, Michael Giacchino, John Williams, Ryuichi Sakamoto, and Jerry Goldsmith. Film music is what initially drew me to the world of composing in its ability to add such profound emotional depth and vivacity to visual mediums. I’m excited to share what I’ve learned on my musical journey so far to any who wish to learn how to play the piano or compose.
