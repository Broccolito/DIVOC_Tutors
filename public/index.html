Mission Statement

Our mission is to help mitigate the public health crisis and fallout caused by the unprecedented COVID-19 pandemic. In light of recent school closures, we understand the quality of education may be compromised. We believe education is one of the key tools to success and therefore should be accessible for all. One of our primary goals is to alleviate the educational implications of COVID-19 by offering free tutoring in a range of subjects scaling up to college level. Our team of tutors are passionate about their studies and deeply invested in giving back to the community by any means possible.

Here at DIVOC Tutors, we provide one-on-one tutoring free of charge. Donations are optional and all proceeds go to programs mitigating the public health crisis caused by COVID-19.
